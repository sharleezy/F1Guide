Haas F1 Team

Haas is a team in Formula 1. They are known to be a mid-field team, so they don't really compete for the top spots, but for maybe top 10. Their current drivers are Kevin Magnussen and Mick Schumacher. You can learn more about the drivers in the "Drivers" section of this guide!
