Zhou Guanyu #24

Guanyu is a 23 year old Chinese driver who also drives for Alfa Romeo alongside Valtteri Bottas. 2022 is his first year in F1, meaning he is currently a rookie. He previously drove in F4, F3, and F2 and achieved many great things while he was there inluding winning the F3 Asian championship. He has now earned his spot in F1, and he is doing very well. He is a talented driver and doing reasonably good for his rookie season. Hopefully we get to see more of him in the future, and he can get his first ever F1 podium!
