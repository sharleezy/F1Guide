Fernando Alonso #14

Fernando is 41 year old Spanish driver who currently drives for Alpine. He is the oldest driver on the grid, and has been racing since 2002, so over 20 years! He actually announced his retirement in 2019, so he did not race in 2020, but he decided to unretire after 2020 and come back. Now we do not know what he is still doing in the sport and cannot wait for him to retire again lol. Just kidding! Alonso is iconic and brings a lot of passion to the sport. He was also World Champion twice in 2005 and 2006. He is was one of Carlos' Sainz role models growing up, and now they're competing together!
