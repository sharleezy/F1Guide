2022 Teams

Learn about each of the 10 Formula One teams of the 2022 season!

To get started, there are 10 teams in Formula 1. Each team has 2 drivers, making there be a total of 20 drivers in Formula One. Teams can also be referred to as "constructors."

While the drivers compete with each other to win their own individual races, the teams overall also compete to win the World Constructors Championship which basically is an award for who built the best car, or who had the best car throughout the season. Each individual Grand Prix also has a constructor winner, and that team is awarded a constructor's trophy.

That means all of the engineers, mechanics, strategists, coaches, etc, are also part of the competition, not just the drivers. These hardworking engineers are always overlooked and should be recognized!

Key/Terms you'll need to know

Base: Where the team is based
Team/Technical Chief: Each team has a team manager and/or CEO that oversees their team. They help make some strategy calls during the race for the drivers because they can also see all the stats of the other drivers and what's going on in the race.
Power Unit: Basically the brand of engine of the F1 car.
First Team Entry: The first year the team drove in F1
World Championships: How many World Championships the team has one
Highest Race Finish: The highest place one of their drivers have finished in a Grand Prix, and how many times
Fastest Laps: The number of times their driver has recorded the fastes lap within an individual Grand Prix
