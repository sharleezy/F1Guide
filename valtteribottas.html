Valtteri Bottas #77

Valtteri is 33 year old Finnish driver who currently drives for Alfa Romeo. He used to drive for Williams from 2013-2016, and he used to be a Mercedes driver from 2017-2021 meaning he was teammates with 7x World Champion Lewis Hamilton. Valtteri is a very talented driver who has great skill and performance, but we all felt bad for him because he was always in Lewis's shadow and never got his shot at winning a title :( Him and Hamilton complimented each other perfectly, but we all still wanted a chance for him. He has 10 race wins and 67 podium finishes. All of his wins being with Mercedes, so you know how talented of a driver he was alongside Lewis.
But now in the 2022 season, he has moved to team Alfa Romeo and is living his best life now. He is a great teammate and mentor to 2022 rookie Zhou Guanyu. If you hear people call him Valtteri "BUTTass," it is because he likes to post him bare bum on social media when he is on vacation.
