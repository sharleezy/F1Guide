Yuki Tsunoda #22

Yuki is 22 year old Japanese driver who currently drives for AlphaTauri. He is the youngest on the grid, and he is also the shortest. A lot of the drivers like to tease him for that, but it works to his advantage since he's so small, he can make his car lighter and go faster. A lot of drivers love him, and he may be known to have some anger issues. However, he can just be very explicit when he makes a mistake during a race which is very understandable. Everybody deals with their emotions in a different way. For such a small frame, he can be very vulgar, but it's okay because he is still Yuki.
