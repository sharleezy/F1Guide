Carlos Sainz #55

Carlos is a 28 year old Spanish driver who currently drives for Ferrari. Personally, I loooveeee Carlos. His character is very charismatic, so he can be friends and hit it off with anyone in the grid. He has special friendships with Lando Norris and also Charles Leclerc, and those friendships are very recognized. Carlos also has some very memeable moments like Charles, for he likes to stare off into the distance a lot. He is very talented, and he got his first pole position of his career this season at Silverstone, and also got his first win of his career this season at Silverstone! He is the first driver to turn his maiden pole to a maiden win.
