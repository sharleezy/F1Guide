Charles Leclerc #16

Charles is 24 year old Monagasque driver who currently drives for Ferrari. Charles is loved by so many people, especially the Tifosi, or Ferrari fans. He is a very popular driver among fans, and he is part of the Twitch quartet along with Alex Albon, George Russell, and Lando Norris. The Twitch quartet would stream on Twitch and play games together during COVID lockdown in 2021. There are many memes of Charles from this era, but he is very memable in general. He also is extrememly talented and is a definite contender for a World Championship title, but he just loses out on so many chances due to Ferrari's bad strategies.
