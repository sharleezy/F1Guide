BWT Alpine F1 Team

Alpine is a new team this season. Last year, they were under the name "Renault." In 2022, Renault decided to rebrand itself to Alpine. Their drivers are Fernando Alonso and Esteban Ocon. You can learn more about the drivers in the "Drivers" section of this guide!
