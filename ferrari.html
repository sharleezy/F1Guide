Scuderia Ferrari

Ferrari is one of the most popular teams in F1. This is the team most-renowned driver Michael Schumacher won his titles for back in the 90s and early 2000s. Their current drivers are Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz, and this season, Ferrari is competing as one of the top 3 teams this season. You can learn more about the drivers in the "Drivers" section of this guide!
