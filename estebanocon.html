Esteban Ocon #31

Esteban is 26 year old French driver who currently drives for Alpine. We like to call him "Estie Bestie" because he's always smiling, and he brings good energy all around. Although he may be enemies with other French driver Pierre Gasly, he is a softie at heart. If you watched him in Drive to Survive, he and Sergio Perez had some issues as teammates, and you owuld have thought he was this big, selfish driver who doesn't know how to drive. But he is also very talented and now he is thriving with his teammate Fernando Alonso!
