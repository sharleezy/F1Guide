Sebastian Vettel #5

Sebastian is 35 year old German driver who currently drives for Aston Martin. Sebastian is an icon because he has been World Champion 4 times in 2010, 2011, 2012, and 2013 with Red Bull. He has been competing since 2007, so he has been in the sport for a very long time. Everyone loves him, and this year he just announced his retirement. This shattered many hearts around the world because he is loved by that many. I like to say he keeps the grid grounded and is many people role models, especially Mick Schumacher.
